About

ROBOTiX CLUB is a ROBOTICS CLUB of NIT Raipur made to enhance and enrich Robotics skills in people having interest in Robotics. Students from numerous colleges throng to to participate in a plethora of events as well as to witness spectacular shows. In a nutshell, ROBOTiX CLUB plays host to a party you would definitely not want to miss out on.

From bird couriers to social networking, humanity has always been trying to spread messages across distances. ROBOTiX_club believes dissipating information is a vital aspect for an organization to thrive. The CA system is an integral part of Inci's Outreach program and also helps Inci identify talented students from colleges. Be a Campus Ambassador and be a part of the culture of NIT Raipur.

Why CA

Opportunity to work for Central India's largest ROBOTiX_club NIT Raipur.
